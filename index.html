WELCOME TO THE HIGH LIZARD SOCIETY


Your favorite cold-blooded reptiles have finally risen from the crevices of the underground.

For too long, they have silently co-existed with humanity, albeit hidden in the shadows, with no recognition for the roles they have played in events that have shaped the annals of society. Great wars, trade deals, global catastrophes, cosmic disturbances – make no mistake, Lizards are the Earth’s true dominant species.

Alas, under the sovereignty of the The Great Sslzard, they have chosen to precipitate an historical shift, by revealing their existence to us warm-blooded folk. They invite us to indulge in their rich yet long-elusive culture, bask in their ever-glorious sun, and share in their supremacy.

Welcoming you – friends, family, newly anointed subjects – to the High Lizard Society.

FAQ

The High Lizard Society (HLS) is a collection of 10,000 Lizards who have risen from the underground after millenia of lurking in the shadows. Holding a Lizard will give members access to a number of benefits including community exclusive events, giveaways, rewards and various utilities upon the activation of our roadmap.

The HLS community will consist of 10,000 Lizards, varying in their degree of rarity.

The mint price for 1 Lizard will be 1.1 Sol

For information on The Great Sslzard please click here

The HLS Store is a virtual marketplace where our community will be able to design and sell their own visual merchandise to other Lizard holders for use on their Lizards. All merchandise must be designed on templates provided by HLS.

The HLS Digital Card will be a premium membership card exclusive to Lizard holders with the goal of transforming digital benefits to real world utility. We aim to partner with various businesses and events worldwide to provide discounts, memberships and special opportunities for HLS members.

Yes, full intellectual properties are given to the buyer of the Lizard.
